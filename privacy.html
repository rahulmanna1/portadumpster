Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2025

At PORTADUMPSTER Toilets (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, billing/shipping address, or other details when you fill out a form or make an inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide services and respond to inquiries

  • Run remarketing and promotional campaigns (you may opt out anytime)

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to understand user behavior, customize content, and serve relevant ads. You may disable cookies via your browser settings, though it may affect site functionality.

4. Sharing Your Data

We may share your information with trusted third parties such as:

  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics)

We ensure these partners are contractually bound to protect your information under applicable data protection laws.

5. Data Protection & Security

We take reasonable measures to secure your data through encryption, secure servers, and regular security checks. However, no system is completely secure, and we encourage caution when sharing sensitive information online.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Correct or update inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal limitations)
  • Opt out of promotional communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@portadumpster.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such data, it will be deleted immediately.
